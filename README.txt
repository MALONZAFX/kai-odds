This is a lightweight front-end demo (pure HTML/CSS/JS) showcasing:
- Dark and Light themes with a toggle (persists to localStorage)
- Glassmorphism UI, mirrored "LIVE ODDS" accents, modern type and spacing
- Sample sport cards and bet slip interactions
- Responsive layout (desktop / mobile)

How to use:
1. Unzip and open index.html in your browser.
2. Toggle theme with the moon/sun icon. Click mini odds to add items to the bet slip (demo only).
